PuzzleModeClient

The PuzzleModeClient Manages the Execution of Puzzle Mode Marking. Puzzle Mode Marking presents the Player with an Array of small known and unknown Images they have to mark in succession. After all Images have been marked, they are sent off to the server, which then evaluates the known images to send back a Score
